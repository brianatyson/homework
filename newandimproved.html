Small Town to Big City

Born in 1986. Abington Memorial Hospital. After shuffling around for awhile, roots were planted in Souderton, Pennsylvania. A small place with a lot of grass, a lot of cows, and that's about sums it up. Souderton is the place everyone comes back to after they graduate from college - safe, quiet, perfect place to raise a family. And I couldn't wait to leave.

I very quickly became an athlete, playing soccer, softball and volleyball and by the time I was in seventh grade I was on four different soccer teams. I ended up riding that wave past high school graduation onto a semi-professional team in Philadelphia, where I played for three years. I declined a fourth year to move out of cowtown into downtown Philadelphia, where I have lived for the past seven years.
